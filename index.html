Know where every dollar is going.

Budgeting is the process of planning how you will spend and save your money. A budget helps you stay in control of your finances, avoid unnecessary debt, and reach your financial goals. Ledger keeps your household's income, spending, and savings in one shared place.

Budgeting tips

  • Make sure you have enough money to cover all expenses, including debt.
  • Expenses should never be greater than your income.
  • Spending more than you earn can lead to debt.
  • Debt means owing money to another person or organization.
  • Planning how you spend your money gives you better financial control.
  • Always build your budget around your net income (after taxes).
